What The Walking Dead Is About

The Walking Dead takes places in Atlanta, Georgia. The show revolves around Rick Grimes(8th on to the left), a sheriff who wakes up in the middle of the apocalypse inside a hospital after getting shot on duty. He then tries to find his family and finds them with a group of survivors. The group tries to find a place to settle down and call home. The problem is either the walkers(zombies) or another rival group led by an evil person like The Governor. Right now, the threat of the group is Negan.
